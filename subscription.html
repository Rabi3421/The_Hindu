Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Hindu Digital access?

The Hindu Digital access is the subscription to get unlimited access to all content available on TheHindu.com on the Web, on desktop and mobile and via Mobile apps available on play store and App Store. This subscription unlocks access to premium features such as personalised article recommendations, quick briefings on the day’s news, access to premium articles that are exclusive to subscribers, and more.Please note: The Hindu current digital subscription packages do not cover Crossword+ or the e-Paper of The Hindu.

Is e-Paper a part of my Digital Subscription?

e-Paper is available with our Readers Choice Combo Plan, Exclusive Combo Pack, and All-Access Pass plan. If you are looking for The Hindu e-Paper standalone pack, please feel free to subscribe to our plans here.

Can I upgrade my plan? What happens to my balance amount and duration?

Yes! You may upgrade your plan to a higher duration plan anytime. You can even move to a recurring plan if you like. You can even upgrade to the "All-in-One and All-Access Plan" which unlocks unlimited access to all the publications of The Hindu Group - The Hindu BusinessLine, Frontline, Sportstar, Crossword Plus and the e-Papers of The Hindu and Businessline. The cost of the plan will be adjusted against the prorated amount of your current plan and duration used.

How to change my password?

You may change or cancel your subscription at any time in your Dashboard in the section “My Plan”
